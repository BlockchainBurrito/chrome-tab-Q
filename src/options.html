Tabs limiter with queue settings

Limit:
Sets maximum of opened tabs. New tabs will be queued.
Pinned tabs and Chrome specific pages won't count on this limit, these are pages like chrome://newtab or chrome-extension://.
NOTE: "Reopen closed tab" will also queue it if the limit has been reached.
New items go to the top of the queue.
Duplicated tabs are not discarded.
When Chrome starts restore queues into different windows.
Disable context menu entries.


Position on the right queue tabs on the right but following a left-to-right order.
Most recent queue tabs starting with the most recently opened.


       Limit:
Sets maximum of loading tabs. If number of current loading tabs reaches this limit, queued items won't be released even if there's enough free spaces.
Pinned tabs and Chrome specific pages won't count for this limit, these are pages like chrome://newtab or chrome-extension://.
